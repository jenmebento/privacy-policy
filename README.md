# Privacy Policy for Bento Ordering Assistant GPT

**Effective Date:** May 18, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the **Bento Ordering Assistant** ("we," "our," or "the GPT") handles personal and order information when you interact with our custom GPT assistant within the OpenAI ChatGPT platform.

We are committed to protecting our customers' privacy and ensuring a transparent, secure ordering experience.

---

## 1. Information We Collect
To process your order inquiries, coordinate logistics, and successfully prepare your meals, the GPT collects the following information provided voluntarily by you during the chat session:
*   **Customer Name**
*   **Contact Phone Number**
*   **Delivery Address**
*   **Order Details:** Estimated item quantities, meal options, specific dietary requirements, and custom logistical notes.
*   **Delivery Logistics:** Requested delivery date and time.

## 2. How We Use Your Information
The information you provide is used strictly for the following operational purposes:
*   To process, confirm, and fulfill your bento meal orders.
*   To communicate with you regarding order logistics, updates, or clarifications.
*   To enable historical "re-order" features for a seamless customer experience.

## 3. Data Storage and Retention
*   **Retention Period:** We securely store your order history and contact details for **one (1) year**. 
*   **Purpose of Retention:** This allows our system to provide you with a convenient "re-order from history" option for future business meetings or gatherings. 
*   **Deletion:** After 1 year, or upon your explicit request, your personal order data will be permanently removed from our active databases.

## 4. Third-Party Data Processing and Sharing
*   **OpenAI Infrastructure:** Because this GPT operates within ChatGPT, your data is processed by OpenAI in accordance with the OpenAI Privacy Policy. 
*   **No Data Selling:** We do not sell, rent, or lease your personal data or order history to third-party marketing companies.
*   **Order Fullfillment:** Data is only transmitted to our secure internal ordering systems and backend APIs to log the transaction and prepare your food.

## 5. Your Rights and Data Control
You maintain full control over your data. You have the right to:
*   Inquire about the specific information we have stored for your account.
*   Request the immediate correction or deletion of your contact and order history before the standard 1-year retention period ends.

## 6. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our ordering operations or backend technical architecture. The "Effective Date" at the top will be updated accordingly.

## Contact Us
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to request the deletion of your ordering history, please contact our support team directly through our official channels.
